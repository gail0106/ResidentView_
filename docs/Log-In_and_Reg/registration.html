PRIVACY NOTICE AND DATA PROTECTION

Data Protection Officer: Barangay Data Protection Office

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL INFORMATION

In accordance with Republic Act 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012), we collect and process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Barangay resident registration and identification
  • Provision of barangay services and programs
  • Emergency contact and disaster preparedness
  • Statistical reporting to local government units
  • Legal compliance with barangay records requirements

TYPES OF INFORMATION COLLECTED

  • Personal identifiers (name, birthdate, birthplace)
  • Contact information (address, email, phone number)
  • Government-issued identification documents
  • Proof of residency documents
  • Account credentials for system access

DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

Your personal information may be shared with:

  • Local Government Units for official purposes
  • Law enforcement agencies when required by law
  • Government statistical offices (in anonymized form)
  • Emergency response agencies during disasters

YOUR RIGHTS AS DATA SUBJECT

Under the Data Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Be informed about the collection and processing of your personal data
  • Access your personal data and request copies
  • Correct or update inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Object to processing of your personal data
  • File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission

DATA SECURITY

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

DATA RETENTION

Personal information will be retained as long as necessary for the purposes stated above and in accordance with legal requirements. Inactive accounts may be archived after 5 years of non-use.
